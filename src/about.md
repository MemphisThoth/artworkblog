# About

This is a blog website about fine art and photography. 

I have been painting for over nine years now, and I wanted to provide knowledge of what I learned over the years.

I am a beginner when it comes to photography, but I am a quick learner, so I want to share what I've learned about that too.

This webiste was originally built with [11ty](https://11ty.dev), and then [Metalsmith](https://metalsmith.io), but I finally settled on [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com).

Jekyll is a static site generator built on ruby. I chose it because it has been around for quite a while, and there is a large community of developers who use it. There are themes you can use to style your Jekyll site, but I decided to create my own styling.
